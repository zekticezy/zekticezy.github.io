A simple, open-source & automatic drum pack unzipper for FL Studio. Automatically unzip your files where they belong. For lazy people, by a lazy person.

For Windows (64/32bit, 10/11) and macOS (M1/M2 processors).











For lazy people, by a lazy person.


[zekkie]'s Fruitpacker is an automatic & simple extractor for FL Studio drum packs (or for other DAWs).

All it takes is a simple drag-and-drop .zip file and you're good to go!









Fruitpacker is free. Forever.


Nothing's locked behind a paywall. Everything is free and accesible to everyone!

If you're feeling generous, then you can support me financially by monetarily helping the project.









Open-source & 100% transparent.

Fruitpacker is open-source software, meaning you can take a look of what's inside the code without worrying about viruses or malware.

Or, you can simply clone the repository and make your own version of Fruitpacker, suited for yourself.

Even if you don't have any coding knowledge, you can help contribute. Fruitpacker is only avaliable in a few langauges, but you can help tranlsate it into yours.
