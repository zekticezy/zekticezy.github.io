Affiliate logins

If you're a zekkie affiliate (video editor, manager, etc.), you have to login here using the @zekkie.dev email assigned to you once you started working with zekkie and it's password.
The login is done completely through Microsoft's Office 365 service, so you'll have access to your calendar, mail and files, directly through the zekkie.dev website, with a few extra
things depending on what position you have.

Problems logging in? (e.g.: forgotten password, invalid credentials, account not yet activated): Get into contact with zekkie here.
